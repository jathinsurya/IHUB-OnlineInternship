Performance Metrics (Week 03)

Model Used:

* YOLOv8n (pretrained)

Dataset:

* Total images processed: 134

  * Video 1: 14 images
  * Video 2: 120 images

Inference-Based Metrics:

* Detection counts per image were analyzed.
* Confidence-based evaluation was limited due to absence of full prediction confidence data.

Observations:

* Higher number of detections in images containing multiple objects.
* Better segmentation quality observed for large and clear objects.
* Reduced performance in low-resolution (640x360) frames.
* Model detects only pretrained classes (e.g., person, car).

Graph Analysis:

* Detection count graph shows variation of objects across frames.
* Higher object density observed in second video frames.

Conclusion:
The segmentation model performs well for common objects and provides precise boundaries, but performance depends on image quality and object visibility.
